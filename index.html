Who we are

A Bible-based, Spirit-filled ministry rooted in Richmond — reaching out to the nations.

Under the leadership of Apostle Dr. Frederick J. Hamilton, Bread of Life Church of Deliverance is a non-denominational, multicultural fellowship in Richmond, California. We gather to worship Jesus Christ, walk in the gifts of the Holy Spirit, and see lives delivered, restored, and called into purpose.

Our work reaches from the Bay Area to East Africa — feeding the hungry, building places of worship, baptizing new believers, and standing publicly for faith in civic life.

Word-rooted

We preach the Bible plainly and let it shape every word we say.

Spirit-led

Signs and wonders, healing, and deliverance — the gifts are still flowing.

Open to all nations

Every people, every tongue — there's a chair at the table for you.

Souls won, lives restored

We serve the East Bay through outreach, prayer, and partnership.

Meet our apostle

Apostle Dr. Frederick J. Hamilton

Senior Apostle & Founding Pastor

Apostle Dr. Frederick J. Hamilton has been called and anointed by God to walk in the office of Apostle — a preacher, teacher, prophet, and pastor who flows in the gifts of the Holy Spirit with signs and wonders following. For decades he has shepherded believers in Richmond and across the East Bay.

"We're not in the business of impressing crowds. We're in the business of seeing lives delivered, families restored, and souls won for the Kingdom."

Beyond the local church, Apostle Hamilton's ministry reaches into international missions — feeding communities, building places of worship, and baptizing new believers in East Africa. His public service has been recognized by the US Global Business Forum and the ICN International Faith-Based Civility Initiative.

After every Sunday service you'll find him at the door, ready to pray with anyone who comes. He'd love to meet you.

— Apostle Dr. Frederick J. Hamilton
